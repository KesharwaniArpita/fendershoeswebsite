About Us

Home / About Us
The FIGARO International was founded in 2003 by Rohit Kumar Mahajan to satisfy the growing demand of companies in need of footwear inventory solutions.
Today, FIGARO International enjoys strong relationships with footwear Traders, mass retailers and brands within the Mainly in North India, ie, Punjab, Haryana, Himachal, Chandigargh, Uttrakhand, Uttar Pradesh.
Allowing us to offer competitively-priced footwear at a India level into emerging markets. We are aggressively expanding our core product lines to include apparel, household goods, cosmetics, and various consumer goods requested by our customers.
We deals in footwear, gents shoes, gents sandals, ladies shoes, ladies sandals, kids shoes, kids sandals, etc.
